Why Air Quality Matters for Schools

Children and adolescents are especially vulnerable to air pollution. During polluted days, exposure to fine particulate matter (PM2.5) increases the risk of respiratory symptoms, fatigue, headaches, and decreased concentration during lessons.

Despite this, many schools do not have clear air quality guidelines. This dashboard was created to help schools, parents, and students make safer daily decisions during periods of poor air quality.
